Millet Upma
Breakfast • 20 min
All ingredients
380 cal 12g protein 58g carbs 12g fat
Ingredients:
Foxtail millet100g
Onions50g
Carrots30g
Green chilies2 pcs
Mustard seeds1 tsp
Curry leaves10 leaves
Oil1 tbsp
Preparation:
  1. Rinse and soak millet for 10 minutes
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves
  3. Add onions, chilies, and carrots. Sauté until soft
  4. Add millet and 2 cups water. Cover and cook for 15 minutes
  5. Fluff with fork and serve hot

Chicken Curry with Vegetables
Dinner • 45 min
All ingredients
460 cal 38g protein 28g carbs 18g fat
Ingredients:
Chicken breast200g
Onions60g
Tomatoes100g
Carrots50g
Garam masala1 tsp
Turmeric½ tsp
Oil1 tbsp
Preparation:
  1. Marinate chicken with turmeric and salt for 30 min
  2. Heat oil, sauté onions until golden
  3. Add tomatoes, cook until soft
  4. Add chicken and vegetables, cook covered for 20 min
  5. Add garam masala, cook for 5 more minutes
